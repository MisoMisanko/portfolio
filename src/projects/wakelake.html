Wakelake Easter Promotion

Role: Chief of Sales and Marketing

The Easter-themed campaign marked a transformation for Wakelake from a party venue into a family-friendly destination. This event kickstarted the season with a unique, engaging concept that combined tradition with sport, attracting families and creating a welcoming atmosphere for a broader audience.

As part of this initiative, I also spearheaded the implementation of a new ticketing system and collaborated with graphic designers to create signature wristbands. These efforts improved branding, streamlined operations, and enhanced the overall customer experience.

Key contributions:

  • Conceptualized and executed the Easter-themed promotional campaign.
  • Collaborated with graphic designers to create branded wristbands that aligned with Wakelake’s new identity.
  • Implemented a new ticketing system to simplify customer check-in and streamline operations.
  • Organized event logistics and partnered with local vendors to ensure a family-friendly atmosphere.

The event received widespread appreciation, establishing Wakelake as a go-to family-friendly venue for the season.
