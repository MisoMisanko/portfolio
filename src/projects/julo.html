About the Campaign

This groundbreaking campaign revived the voice of legendary Slovak actor and comedian Julo Satinský using AI technology. In collaboration with his family, we ensured the ethical use of his voice by restricting it to readings from his own book, allowing his legacy to live on respectfully.

My Role

  • Campaign Contributor: Collaborated on one of the most successful marketing campaigns in Slovakia where we revived Julo Satinský's legacy using AI.
  • Debate Curator: Designed and managed the "Debates Without Stubbornness" series, bringing together prominent figures from opposing views to discuss critical societal topics:
    • Catholic Bishop Róbert Bezák vs. Comedian Fero Joke on Church vs. LGBTQ
    • Sociologist Michal Vašečka vs. Informatics Professor Mária Bieliková on AI & Robots vs. Human Labor
    • Priest Ondrej Prostredník vs. Michaela Chrkavá on Abortion
