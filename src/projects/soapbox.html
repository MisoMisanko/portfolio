Red Bull Soapbox Race

Role: Team Leader & Designer

Participated in the Red Bull Soapbox Race in London, leading a team to design and build a soapbox car for the event. The project involved creativity, problem-solving, and team coordination under strict time constraints.

Key contributions:

  • Designed the soapbox car, ensuring it met the technical and aesthetic requirements of the race.
  • Collaborated with teammates to source materials, build the car, and manage logistics.
  • Focused on safety and performance while maintaining a unique and eye-catching design.
  • Delivered a competitive entry that showcased teamwork, ingenuity, and execution.

The event was an unforgettable experience that demonstrated my ability to combine creativity and collaboration in a challenging, high-energy environment.
