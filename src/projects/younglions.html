Young Lions Bronze Winner

Role: Creative Lead & Copywriter

This award-winning project for the **Young Lions competition** involved crafting an ad campaign that highlighted Bratislava's unique charm through a bold and locally-driven concept. We earned **Bronze** for our creative approach to inviting tourists to explore the city through the eyes of its residents.

Key contributions:

  • Developed the campaign concept, showcasing Bratislava’s cultural appeal.
  • Worked collaboratively with the team to ensure the message aligned with the competition's goals.
  • Scripted the ad copy and ensured the visual storytelling resonated with the audience.

This project highlights my ability to deliver creative, impactful campaigns under pressure, blending local insights with a global vision.
