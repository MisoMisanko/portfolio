About the Project

Recognized with a Bronze award at the prestigious Young Lions competition for creating a film campaign that invited tourists to Bratislava. The campaign was crafted by locals to showcase the city’s charm authentically and innovatively.

My Role

  • Concept Creator: Developed the campaign’s concept and crafted the script for the film.
  • Creative Collaboration: Worked with local talent to ensure authenticity and cultural relevance.
