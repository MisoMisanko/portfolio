Zaraguza PF Campaign – Effie Prize

Role: Ideator & Project Manager

The Zaraguza PF Campaign celebrated the agency’s first Effie Grand Prix by turning it into a memorable symbol of collaboration. The trophy was cut into small pieces and sent to our partners as a gesture of shared success, reinforcing the idea that great achievements come from teamwork.

Key contributions:

  • Conceptualized the campaign, ensuring the idea aligned with Zaraguza’s branding and values.
  • Collaborated with designers and production teams to create custom packaging for the trophy pieces.
  • Coordinated distribution logistics to ensure timely delivery to partners.
  • Received recognition in Slovak advertising for its creative execution and impactful message.

This campaign stood out as a unique and meaningful way to thank our collaborators and highlight the importance of partnership in achieving success.
